About Us

Hello! Our name is Diamond S Custom Grazing, and we specialize in, well, custom grazing. If you don't know what custom grazing is, it is where animals graze in little sections on a field called paddocks. During their time in each paddock, they finish off the grass until there is little left, and when they are moved in a few days, the grass is allowed to grow back better (because of the manure). When their time is up (~3 months), they will look better, and so will the land. So it's a win-win situation.

We were founded in 2020 in Ruby Valley, Nevada.
