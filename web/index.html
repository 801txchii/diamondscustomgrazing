Welcome to Diamond S Custom Grazing!

What We Offer

We offer custom grazing, which is where a piece of land is divided into parts called paddocks. The animals stay in each paddock for 3 days, and then moved to the next one. During there time in each paddock, they have to finish off the grass. When they are moved, this allows the previous paddock to regrow better, due to all of the manure. This method of grazing is not only better for the land but makes the animals gain weight faster. So it's a win-win situation.
